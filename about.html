NÉO ÓRAMA

Sabine Camhi

Sabine Camhi is a designer and copywriter based in Athens, Greece. She specializes in repurposing old furniture and upcycling materials such as wood and plastics. In her recent work, she combines reclaimed wood with sisal fibre from her family's farm in Kenya to create refined design pieces.

Nick Trian

Nick Trian is an artist and designer based in Serifos island, Greece. He studied Furniture & Interior Design at the University of Plymouth. Nick excels in collecting discarded products, utilizing knives, saws, and drills to craft his exquisite, one-of-a-kind artworks.
