Sabine Camhi

Sabine Camhi was born in Athens in 1998. Despite a business background, she discovered her passion for furniture and sculpture design during the pandemic. Sabine's artistic focus revolves around repurposing found objects. In her recent work, she blends reclaimed wood with sisal from her family's farm in Kenya, resulting in vibrant lighting designs that embody her commitment to sustainability and creativity.

Nick Trian

Nikolas Triantafyllidis is a visionary artist from Athens, Greece born in 1990. With a background in Furniture & Interior Design, he seamlessly blends design and sculpture in his Serifos Island studio. Nikolas crafts unique pieces fusing several materials and techniques, pushing the boundaries of innovation at the intersection of design and artistry.
